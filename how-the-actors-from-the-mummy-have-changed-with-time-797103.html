How the Actors From The Mummy Have Changed With Time

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, July 15, 2024

The Mummy (1999) and The Mummy Returns (2001) were box office hits that fully satisfied our hunger for adventure. These movies had everything to win an army of fans — a good cast, the atmosphere of ancient Egypt, action, intrigue, and a captivating plot. Both of these movies are still interesting and exciting, and it’s hard to believe they were shot 2 decades ago.

Here at Bright Side, we want to remember the characters from these hit movies, and show you how they’ve changed with time.

Rachel Weisz (Evelyn Carnahan)

Arnold Vosloo (Imhotep)

Jonathan Hyde (Dr. Allen Chamberlain)

Oded Fehr (Ardeth Bay)

Patricia Velasquez (Anck-su-namun)

Dwayne Johnson (The Scorpion King)

Alun Armstrong (Mr. Hafez)

Freddie Boath (Alex O’Connell)
