What 31 Beauty Icons of the 20th Century Look Like Today

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 26, 2024

A few of these weren't their best current photos. Seeing interviews or current movies really show how many of the women are still breathtaking with warm smiles. I may be mostly taking about Julie Andrews. I adore her! Carol Burnett should be on this list. Saw her at the Ryman a couple years ago. She was still witty with a touch of sass. Her memory is impeccable. Maybe some didn't consider her a beauty icon, but I thought she's always been a uniquely beautiful, hilarious woman.

I also adore how many of the older beauty icons have embraced their wrinkles. They make me want to listen to stories about making movies and everything they've seen in the world.
