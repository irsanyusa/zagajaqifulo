Denmark Lady Wears Trad Outfit at Wedding, Asks to Be Sprayed Money, Many React: &quot;She Learnt Fast&quot;

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 17, 2024
  • A Denmark lady Cici showed she had adapted to the pattern at Nigerian weddings as she wore a green traditional outfit to the occasion
  • She combined her attire with a green 'gele', which she accessorised with earrings and a necklace that gave her a gorgeous look
  • The lady was a family member of the bride and was asked to read out the engagement letter, which she did interestingly

A Denmark lady Cici (@cici.onthemove on Instagram) got the reactions of netizens after a video of her beautiful outfit and her actions at a wedding trended online.

The lady rocked a green lace outfit and tied a 'gele', which looked stunning on her. She also wore silver earrings and a necklace, which complemented her attire.

Being a family member of the bride, identified as Anne, who was being joined in holy matrimony to Fisayo, her Nigerian groom, the German lady was asked to read the engagement letter.
