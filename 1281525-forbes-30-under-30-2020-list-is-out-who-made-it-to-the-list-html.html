Forbes 30 Under 30 2020 list out. Who made it to the list?

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Every year, Forbes releases its 'Forbes 30 Under 30' list. This list comprises of young minds who have taken great strides in the selected twenty categories. These young risk-takers, game changers and entrepreneurs show the world that when it comes to great minds, hard work and success, age is just a number.

This year, the magazine received over fifteen thousand (15,000) submissions on who should be included in this coveted list on names. Despite the numerous suggestions, the Forbes 30 Under 30 list is comprised of six hundred (600) names. These innovators and trendsetters are divided into twenty categories with each category consisting of thirty names.

Forbes 30 Under 30 2020

Listed below are the top five names of people who made the list in each category. For the full list of names, check out the official Forbes website. The full list of names has 21% of people based in New York, followed by San Francisco (17%), Los Angeles (12%) and Boston (6%).

Art & Style

This list is made up of artists, photographers, illustrators, fashion and jewellery designers. The judges for this list were Tory Burch, Sarah Staudinger and Kehinde Wiley

  • Sarah Abbasi - The 29-year-old fashion designer has an eco-friendly fashion brand, 'sahroo'. The bridal brand has all its clothing items made by hand in Pakistan.
  • Tunji Adeniyi-Jones - The New-York based artist draws inspiration from West Africa for his painting. His incredible paintings are shown to feature curvaceous figures and vibrant colours. They incorporate the ancient history and mythology of West Africa.
  • Farah Al Qasimi - Born in the United Arab Emirates, the photographer uses her talents to showcase postcolonial structures of power, gender and taste in the Gulf Arab states. She is 28-years old.
  • Danny Casale - At 24, Danny, also known as 'Coolman Coffeedan', is a New-York based illustrator. His works can be found on his YouTube Channel (where he has over a million subscribers) as well as on his official website.
  • Yixi Chen - In 2018, 25-year-old Yixi brought in a revenue of $3 million from her clothing line C2H4. Now at 26, she has made her debut in the 30 Under 30 list as a 'call-out'.

Consumer Technology
