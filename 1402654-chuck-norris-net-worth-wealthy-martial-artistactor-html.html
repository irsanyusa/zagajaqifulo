Chuck Norris net worth: how wealthy is the martial artist/actor?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 2, 2024

If you are a great fan of action movies, then Chuck Norris' name is not new. He is a well known Hollywood star who has captured many fans' hearts from his action tricks in various films. He is also a film producer, screenwriter, author and a martial arts expert. Throughout his career, he has accumulated a lot of wealth. A look at Chuck Norris net worth will help us understand how rich he is.

Carlos Ray Norris, aka Chuck Norris, was born on March 10th 1940, in Ryan, Oklahoma, USA, to Wilma and Ray Norris.

His parents divorced when he was a teenager, and his father walked out of his life. He then helped his mother raise his younger brothers.

As a famous martial arts expert, he has a black belt in Taekwondo, Karate, Judo, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and even own school of fighting, called Chun Kuk Do.
