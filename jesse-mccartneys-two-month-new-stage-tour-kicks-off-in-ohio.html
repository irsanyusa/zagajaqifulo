Jesse McCartney has officially returned to the stage following his tour postponement. After months of anticipation, fans will finally be able to attend this tour headlining the singer’s newest release. The singer kicks off his rescheduled New Stage tour on May 3.

Jesse McCartney embarks on the New Stage tour on May 3 in Cincinnati, Ohio. This tour originally came to light following the release of his album, New Stage. This tour was first teased on Instagram in July 2021. Within the singer’s post, he welcomed fans to his in-person tour beginning in November, ending in December. At this time, McCartney also announced Jamie Miller and Casey Baer as openers for the concert series.

Soon after the albums release McCartney shared his team’s choice to postpone the concert series in October. At the time, he noted that the delta variant made it not safe to host indoor events. The singer promised to reschedule the tour in the coming spring. In early March, McCartney and his team announced rescheduled dates for the tour.

Jesse McCartney Kicks off The New Stage Tour 2022

His current tour features 34 cities beginning in May in Ohio and ending in June in California. To kick off the tour in the best way possible, McCartney appeared on The Kelly Clarkson Show on May 3. Within his appearance he sang the sixth track “Selfless” off of New Stage with his band.

Tickets are now on sale for this two-month concert series. Prices for tickets are dependent for the venue but lie within the $25 to $499. There are two VIP packages available for McCartney’s upcoming concert series.

The first package, the My “Friends” meet and greet package features VIP entry, exclusive after show meet and greet, personal photo, merchandise, and a laminate. The other package, the All “Yours” Elite Meet and Greet features VIP entry, a pre-show hang with the singer, meet and greet after this show, a personal photo, merchandise, and more. Check local venue listings for the meet and greet package prices.
