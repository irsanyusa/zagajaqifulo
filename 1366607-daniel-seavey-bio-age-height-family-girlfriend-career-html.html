Career

Daniel began learning music at the age of seven. He plays various musical instruments including, the guitar, cello, piano, bass, mandolin, drums, piano, and ukulele.

As a little boy, Daniel would travel to Portland with his father to play music on the streets for art walks. At the age of 15, he auditioned for a chance to compete on American Idol, and luckily, he got his chance.

Daniel auditioned for season 14 of American Idol in 2015. His rendition of Paula Abdul's Straight Up and Leonard Cohen's Hallelujah at his audition in San Francisco earned him a ticket to Hollywood.

At the Hollywood rounds, he sang Thinking Out Loud, and I See Fire by Ed Sheeran to advance to the Showcase stage.

During the Showcase Week, he sang Straight Up by Paula Abdul again and earned a spot in the Top 24 where he sang I'm Yours by Jason Mraz.

In 2016, Seavey joined the boy band Why Don't We. The five-person band released their first single, Taking You, in October 2016. They released their first EP Only the Beginning on November 25, 2016.

The band released three more EPs, Something Different, Why Don’t We, and Invitation.

The band went on their Invitation tour, and in December, they performed on Jingle Ball 2017 in front of more than 20,000 people.

The band released its first full-length studio album, 8 Letter, on August 31, 2018, and the album made the top ten on the Billboard pop chart.
