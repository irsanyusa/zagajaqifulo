Gabby Windey Get Candid About 'DWTS' Wardrobe Malfunction

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 25, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

Good Morning America

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Dancing with the Stars is one of the most beloved television shows in America, and it takes a lot of work to ensure that everything goes smoothly on camera. However, behind every perfectly executed dance routine, there are a million things that can go wrong. Recently, former contestant Gabby Windey opened up about a wardrobe malfunction she experienced while on the show.

Windey may have won over viewers with her impressive dance moves on DWTS Season 31, but things weren’t always picture-perfect behind the scenes. In an Instagram Q&A, the reality star revealed that she experienced a wardrobe malfunction during one of her performances.

The incident occurred while she was performing a low-cut dress, and the show’s wardrobe team had to sew extra fabric onto her dress at the last minute to protect her from any potential nip slips. According to Windey, the wardrobe team was always present to ensure that contestants were wearing pasties and other protective gear.

Despite their efforts, however, Windey still experienced a wardrobe malfunction in the low-cut dress during one of her performances. At the last minute, the team had to sew extra fabric onto her dress to prevent any mishaps.

While wardrobe malfunctions are always a possibility on DWTS, Windey’s experience serves as a reminder of just how much work goes into ensuring that everything runs smoothly on camera. From the show’s wardrobe team to the dancers themselves, everyone involved works tirelessly to put on the best show possible for viewers at home.

She Was Involved With Pros Vinny Guadagnino, Alan Bersten

Windey has been at the center of dating rumors involving two fellow dancers from the show. Windey recently spoke about her relationship with Guadagnino, stating that while they talk consistently and have fun together, their situation remains unclear. She described Guadagnino as a good friend and admitted that he has flirted with her.

However, Windey has also been spotted on a dinner date with another DWTS pro, Alan Bersten. The pair danced together during the previous season when Bersten filled in for Windey’s original partner. Reports suggest that Windey and Bersten appeared to be enjoying each other’s company during their date, with hopes that something more may develop between them.

As the rumors continue to swirl, fans eagerly await further developments in Windey’s love life. Will she find love with Guadagnino or pursue a relationship with Bersten? Only time will tell.
