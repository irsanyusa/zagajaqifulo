20+ Famous Heartthrobs Who Look Way Different With a Beard

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, May 26, 2024

We know that actors change their looks often and sometimes become unrecognizable. Makeup artists and special effect specialists can make a person look much younger or much older, turn them into a magical creature, or make them look like a completely different person. But sometimes something as simple as a haircut, beard, or mustache can significantly change a man’s appearance.

At Bright Side, we found famous actors who aren’t afraid to experiment with their appearance, both on movie sets and in real life. And in the bonus section, you’ll see how Nick Offerman changes his looks like a chameleon.
