Nicole Kidman Reveals the Secret to Her Age-Defying Looks: I Tried Botox, but I Got Out of It

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 27, 2024

At age 55, Nicole Kidman stuns everyone with her glowing skin. Even though it might be hard to believe, she claims that her seemingly ageless looks are not the result of any cosmetic enhancements. The actress recently revealed what some of her secrets are for aging like fine wine, and we decided to share them with you.

Kidman claims her face is completely natural.

Kidman revealed she has never had any surgeries on her face, but in the past, she admits to using Botox. However, the star stopped using it because she didn’t like how her face looked afterward and because she couldn’t move anything on it. “I did try Botox, unfortunately, but I got out of it, and now I can finally move my face again,” she explained.

She believes wearing sunscreen plays a big part in her youthful skin.

AF Archive/Graham Whitby Boot/Mary Evans Picture Library/East News

Kidman revealed that because of her pale complexion, she started using sunscreen regularly when she was a kid. She also explained that she can’t go in the sun that much because she is fair-skinned. And even though that was a nightmare when she was younger, it certainly brought her a lot of benefits later on.

Kidman doesn’t judge people who undergo procedures to look younger, but personally, she believes in being physically fit.

Kidman admitted that she has tried a lot of things to look younger but later realized that, aside from good nutrition and exercise, most things don’t make a lot of difference. “I take care of myself, and I am proud to say that,” she explained.

She also makes sure to take her makeup off and cleans her skin twice every night.

A double-cleansing routine every night might sound like a lot of work and impossible to manage for many. But that’s the way Kidman likes to do it. So when she is having a late night or has been traveling and can’t follow her usual routine, she opts for a cleansing wipe to make things easy and quick.
