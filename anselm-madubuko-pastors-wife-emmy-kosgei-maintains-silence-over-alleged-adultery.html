Pastor's wife Emmy Kosgei maintains silence over alleged adultery story

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, June 23, 2024

According to the NET, Pastor Anselm Madubuko confessed to having an affair with Kikelomo Akinkunmi at his church last Sunday. Both parties have not commented on this story. She has been tweeting today but has not made mention of the story.

The wife of the Revival Assembly leader Emmy Kosgei hasn’t spoken about the story either. All her tweets so far today (October 21) are not related to the story.

Pastor Anselm Madubuko and Emmy Kosgei got married in 2013. The pastor’s first wife Pastor Connie Madubuko died in 2012.

Emmy Kosgei was born in 1980, and is said to be one of Kenya's popular gospel singers. She has a school in Kenya called the Hope Academy.
